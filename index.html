About

Engineering AI systems from research insight to production reality.

AI Engineer with a PhD in Computer Science, I have 8 years of experience designing and building innovative artificial intelligence solutions. I specialize in architecting and industrializing AI systems, turning complex prototypes into scalable services. My expertise includes cutting-edge frameworks such as LangGraph and LangChain, microservices orchestration, and the rigorous application of software engineering principles to ensure code quality, reliability, and long-term maintainability.

My technical scope covers critical areas such as securing RAG pipelines, time series analysis, and fraud detection. I support data-driven projects across their full lifecycle, from business needs to deployment, and I am also the author of scientific papers and a patent.
